Welcome to Doyen Prestige Electricals

As an electrical contracting firm with an expandable Union workforce, we handle electrical contracting jobs, ranging from residential to industrial facilities. Most of our work is in new construction, but we are also experienced in rehabbing and retrofitting buildings with the latest lighting, wireless and security systems.
We provide a full range of electrical services from the beginning stages of a project such as design/build or value-engineering, to building maintenance, electrical services and emergency service electrical calls.
We work with local and remote general contractors, owners and developers to provide competitive electrical quotes for all types of commercial, residential, industrial and institutional electrical projects

About Us

CEO

BRIGHT OSEI-OWUSU

Bright Osei-Owusu manages field and warehouse operations for Doyen Prestige Electricals.

Doyen Prestige Electricals is a premier electrical contractor for the construction industry. We specialize in building relationships with our partners, providing exceptional customer service with courtesy and trust. Our knowledge and experience provides you stability, speed and consistency and our company values ensure honesty and integrity.

Doyen Prestige Electricals & finishing was founded in 2013 by Bright Osei-Owusu but incorporated as a limited liability company in 2019. Armed with a few contacts, some residential work, and a positive work ethic, they steadily grew the business into a giant in a short time. From an office in Kumasi, Ashanti region, they service general contractors, developers and business owners throughout Ghana.

Doyen Prestige Electricals maintains a core group of excellent electricians, some who have been with the company since its inception in 2013. With workplace happiness a core business essential, Doyen Prestige Electricals provides staff training and opportunities for advancement to its employees. We hire qualified electricians, apprentices and apprentice trainees and help train, groom and advance them through to the foremen and project manager levels.
